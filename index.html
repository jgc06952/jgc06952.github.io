My mom used to follow a recipe for this, but we made it together so much that I have pretty much memorized the measurements (or I do whatever feels right!). You need any shape of pasta, a jar of roasted red peppers, heavy cream, basil, garlic, salt, pepper, and a little LOTS of parmesan cheese.

I have made this dish so many times for me and my friends at college, and it is always a hit. Cooking for people is one of my favorite things to do, because it is so fun and it brings people together!
